Welcome to Eyeris

Real-time Eye detection using the latest AI technology.

    About

    This Eyeris application uses advanced AI models to detect and visualize facial landmarks in real-time. It leverages the MediaPipe FaceLandmarker task to provide accurate and efficient face landmark detection. Whether you are a developer looking to integrate facial recognition into your app or a user curious about how AI sees your face, this tool offers a glimpse into the future of interactive computer vision.
